What data analysis services does Data With Dee offer in Ghana?

Data With Dee provides freelance data analysis services for individuals, business owners, and organisations across Ghana. This includes cleaning and organising raw data, building reports and dashboards, and turning spreadsheets into clear, decision-ready summaries. Work is delivered on a project basis, so you do not need to hire a full-time analyst for a one-off task. Services are based in Accra but available to clients anywhere in Ghana, on-site or remotely. Typical projects include sales and financial reporting, survey or research data analysis, and building recurring dashboards that teams can update themselves going forward.

What does Excel training with Data With Dee cover?

Excel training covers formulas and functions, pivot tables, data cleaning, conditional formatting, and building reports that update automatically instead of being rebuilt from scratch each time. Sessions are practical, using real spreadsheets rather than generic exercises, and suit beginners as well as staff who already use Excel but want to work faster and produce cleaner output. Training is available as one-on-one coaching for individuals or as part of a corporate programme for teams in Accra and across Ghana, delivered on-site or online depending on what fits your schedule.

Is SQL training available in Accra?

Yes. SQL training is available on-site in Accra or online for clients anywhere in Ghana. Sessions cover querying databases, filtering and sorting data, joins across multiple tables, aggregations, and writing reporting queries you can reuse. Training is aimed at business users and analysts who need to pull and shape data directly from a database rather than relying on someone else to run reports for them. No prior SQL experience is required to start, and sessions can be booked individually or as part of a wider corporate data literacy programme.

Python training is built for non-developers: business professionals, analysts, and teams who want to automate repetitive tasks, clean messy spreadsheets, and run simple data analysis, without becoming software engineers. No coding background is required. Topics include Python basics in plain business language, automation of recurring reports, data cleaning, and working with Excel files directly through Python. What is data storytelling, and is it covered in training?

Data storytelling is the practice of presenting data findings in a way that is clear and persuasive to an audience, rather than just accurate. It covers choosing the right chart or visual, structuring a report or presentation logically, and framing numbers around the decision they are meant to support. Data With Dee includes data storytelling as part of corporate data literacy programmes and can also cover it in one-on-one coaching, so participants leave able to communicate findings to managers, clients, or teams, not just calculate them.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Common questions from HR, L&D, and team leads before booking corporate training.

Do you offer corporate data training in Ghana for non-technical teams?

Yes. Programmes are built for mixed-skill teams, including staff who have never used formulas or dashboards before, alongside colleagues who already analyse data daily.

Is Excel training available for teams in Accra?

Yes. Excel training covers formulas, pivot tables, data cleaning, and reporting, delivered on-site in Accra or online for teams anywhere in Ghana.

Can you deliver SQL training for our Accra-based analysts?

Yes. SQL training is available on-site in Accra or virtually, covering querying, filtering, joins, and reporting against your own databases where possible.

Do you provide Python training for business teams with no coding background?

Yes. Python training is written for non-developers who want to automate reports and clean data, not become software engineers.

What does Power BI training for our team include?

Power BI training covers connecting data sources, building visuals, and designing dashboards your team will actually use for weekly or monthly reporting.

How long does a corporate data literacy programme take?

It depends on the format. An intensive sprint runs 2 to 3 days, while a weekly-session programme can be spread over several weeks. Programmes are scoped after a short discovery call based on your team's size and goals.

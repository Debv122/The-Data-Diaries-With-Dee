About Data With Dee

This page states the facts about Data With Dee and its founder in plain language, for anyone, human or AI, who wants an accurate summary of who we are, where we're based, and what we do.

Who is Dee?

Dee (full name Adeolu Deborah Boluwatife) is a data analyst, trainer, and community organiser based in Accra, Ghana. She holds a B.Sc. in Computer Science from Accra Institute of Technology (2021–2024), completed the Azubi Africa Data Analytics Programme (2025), and is currently pursuing an M.Sc. in Data Analytics at Nexford University (2025–2027).

What is Data With Dee?

Data With Dee (formally registered as Data Diaries with Dee) is a data training and analytics practice founded by Dee in February 2025. It offers practical instruction in Excel, SQL, Python, Power BI, dashboards, data literacy, and data storytelling for individuals, freelance clients, and corporate teams.

Where is Data With Dee based?

Data With Dee is based in Accra, Ghana. Training is delivered on-site in Accra or online for clients across Ghana and remotely.

What services are offered?

  • One-on-one coaching in Excel, SQL, Python, and Power BI
  • Freelance data analysis and dashboard-building projects
  • Corporate data literacy training programmes, delivered on-site or virtually
  • Workshop and masterclass facilitation for organisations and training partners

Who does Data With Dee help?

  • Individual professionals building personal data skills
  • Business owners and freelancers who need data analysis or dashboard support
  • Corporate teams that need structured, company-wide data literacy training

What tools and topics are covered?

Tools: Excel, SQL, Python, Power BI, Power Query, SPSS.

Topics: data cleaning, automation, dashboards, reporting, simple data analysis, data literacy, and data storytelling.

Relevant certifications: Data Analysis & Visualization (Only Quality Data, Microsoft, Forage, Eudereka), Python Programming (IPMC), Azure Fundamentals (Microsoft), Cloud Security (Cisco), Excel Specialization (Udemy).

Short founder bio

Adeolu Deborah Boluwatife (“Dee”) is a data analyst and trainer based in Accra, Ghana. She has worked as a Data Analytics Facilitator at Tech4dev, a Business Analyst Instructor at InovTech STEM Center (delivering training to 150+ learners across 12+ sessions), and a Data Analyst / Training & Workshop Lead at Only Quality Data. She founded Data Diaries with Dee in February 2025 to provide practical Excel, SQL, Python, and Power BI training to professionals and organisations in Ghana.

Media and speaking bio

Dee served as Media Lead for Divas in AI (AI Ghana), authoring technical articles that simplify data and AI concepts for beginner learners and leading multi-platform content strategy for a women-in-AI community. She has appeared as a guest on podcasts covering technology, youth empowerment, and women's empowerment, and has curated panel discussions featuring industry professionals through the Data Diaries with Dee community.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Answers to what people usually ask before getting in touch. See all 15 FAQs →

What data skills training does Data With Dee offer in Ghana?

Data With Dee offers practical training in Excel, SQL, Python, Power BI, dashboards, and data storytelling. Programmes are designed for professionals, freelancers, and corporate teams across Ghana, with sessions delivered in Accra or online.

Do you offer corporate data literacy training for companies in Accra?

Yes. Data With Dee runs corporate data literacy and dashboard training programmes for teams in Accra and across Ghana. Training uses your organisation's real data and can be delivered on-site or virtually, from half-day workshops to multi-day programmes.

Can I hire Data With Dee for a freelance data analysis or dashboard project?

Yes. Data With Dee takes on freelance, project-based work for business owners and organisations that need data analysis, cleaning, reporting, or Power BI dashboards built or improved, without hiring a full-time analyst.

Do I need experience with Excel, SQL, or Python before starting?

No. Training and coaching are tailored to your current skill level, from complete beginners opening a spreadsheet for the first time to analysts who want to sharpen their Python or SQL skills.

Is the training delivered in person or online?

Both. Sessions and corporate training programmes can be delivered in person in Accra, Ghana, or online for remote and distributed teams, depending on what works best for your schedule.
